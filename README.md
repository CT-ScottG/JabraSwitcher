# Jabra Switcher

Ever wished you could turn off your Jabra headset and have your computer
automatically switch back to your speakers, without having to unplug the USB
dongle? Jabra Switcher is a simple utility that does just that. 

Using the Jabra SDK, it monitors the connection status of your Jabra headset
and automatically switches your default audio device when it is turned on or
off.
